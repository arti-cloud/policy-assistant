EPF (Provident Fund): Employer contributes 12% of eligible wages; statutory scheme benefits like EPS/EDLI included.

ESI (State Insurance): For employees with wages ≤ ₹21,000/month. Contributions: Employer 3.25%, Employee 0.75%.

Gratuity: Payable after 5 years of continuous service or earlier if death/disablement; capped at ₹20 lakh (as per Act).

Bonus Act: Statutory bonus between 8.33% – 20% for eligible employees. Wage ceiling ₹21,000/month.

Health Insurance: ₹5 lakh family floater coverage with OPD and wellness benefits. Optional top-up at employee cost.

Learning & Development: Budget for training & certifications aligned to role, with manager’s approval.
